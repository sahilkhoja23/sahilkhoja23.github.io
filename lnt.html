Leave No Trace.

Design Meets Sustainability.

Overview

Leave No Trace is a mobile application that allows users to track their energy usage and reduce their overall carbon footprint. The concept was awarded 'Best in Nation' in the Verizon Innovative Application Challenege by the Verizon Foundation. The award gave us a grant of $20,000 to develop the application, and we partnered with MIT's Media Lab and University of Texas - Dallas to build the product.

Why?

I wanted to keep the experience of the app visual and simple for several reasons. Based on surveys and user testing we conducted, many people didn't know how to parse their energy bills and then transfer that information to our app. Therefore, we kept the interface with a very limited amount of variables, and even gave users to shorten the different types of energy usage that they had to manually input. In addition, the trends are best described by line graphs, typically used whenever dealing with changes over time. I still think that giving users some sort of swipe tutorial or tooltips would help explain energy units that people aren't familiar with, but its something we can add in the 2.0!
